## When to Use

Use this checklist for any loan file where gift funds are being used toward:
- Down payment
- Closing costs
- Reserves (family member donors only)

Applies to all occupancy types except investment properties (gifts are not permitted for investment properties).

---

## Required Documentation

- [ ] **Gift letter** signed and dated by **both** donor and borrower, containing all of the following:
  - [ ] Dollar amount of the gift
  - [ ] Date the funds were (or will be) transferred
  - [ ] Donor's name, address, and telephone number
  - [ ] Donor's relationship to the borrower
  - [ ] Statement that no repayment is expected or required, in any form (cash or future services)

- [ ] **Evidence of donor's ability to give**: most recent bank statement(s) for the donor's account showing the funds were available prior to transfer

- [ ] **Evidence of transfer and receipt** — one of the following:
  - [ ] Borrower's bank statement showing the gift deposit (preferred)
  - [ ] Wire transfer confirmation or certified check copy, if funds are being provided at closing
  - [ ] Settlement statement (HUD-1 or CD) showing gift funds applied at closing, if wired directly to closing agent

---

## Conditional Documentation

- [ ] **If gift amount appears as a large deposit on borrower's bank statements**: also satisfy [[large-deposits]] documentation requirements — written explanation and sourcing documentation
- [ ] **If gift donor is an employer or has any financial interest in the transaction**: do not treat as a gift — reclassify and analyze under [[def-interested-party-contribution|interested party contribution]] rules
- [ ] **If gift is a gift of equity** (seller is a family member crediting equity): use gift of equity documentation instead — see [[gift-of-equity]]
- [ ] **If the gift has not yet been deposited at time of underwriting**: document expected transfer method (certified check, wire) and confirm receipt before closing
- [ ] **If gift funds are being used for reserves**: confirm donor is a family member; gift funds from non-family donors are not acceptable as reserves

---

## Donor Eligibility Verification

Before using this checklist, confirm the donor is an eligible party under Fannie Mae guidelines:

- [ ] Relative (spouse, child, parent, sibling, grandparent, aunt, uncle, or their spouse)
- [ ] Domestic partner
- [ ] Fiancé or fiancée
- [ ] Godparent or godchild (confirm lender policy — not explicitly listed in all Selling Guide versions)
- [ ] **NOT**: friend, employer (in most cases), builder, real estate agent, or other interested party
