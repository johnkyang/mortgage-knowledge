_The loanDepot Conventional Guide does not have a dedicated Grants/DPA section. DPA programs are referenced within [[secondary-subordinate-financing]] (all Community Seconds/Affordable Seconds/DPA programs must be listed in the AFH Program Directory) and [[minimum-borrower-contribution]] (FHLMC: grants trigger a 5% own-funds requirement for second homes at LTV > 80%). Full DPA guidelines must be sourced from the Fannie Mae Selling Guide (B5-5.1-02 Community Seconds) or Freddie Mac Seller Guide (4204.2 Affordable Second)._
