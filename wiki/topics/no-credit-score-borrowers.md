## Overview

Loans where no borrower has a credit score are eligible under limited circumstances per loanDepot policy. AUS approval is required; manual underwriting is not permitted. (Lender note: Source: [[source-loandepot-conventional-guide-2026-03]], p.313)

---

## Key Rules

### Universal Requirements (All GSEs)

- (Lender note: Maximum 95% LTV/CLTV/HCLTV) (Source: [[source-loandepot-conventional-guide-2026-03]], p.313)
- (Lender note: Must be a principal residence; all borrowers must occupy the property) (Source: [[source-loandepot-conventional-guide-2026-03]], p.313)
- (Lender note: Transaction must be a purchase or limited/no cash-out refinance) (Source: [[source-loandepot-conventional-guide-2026-03]], p.313)
- (Lender note: Conforming loan amounts only) (Source: [[source-loandepot-conventional-guide-2026-03]], p.313)
- (Lender note: The loan must be a fixed-rate mortgage) (Source: [[source-loandepot-conventional-guide-2026-03]], p.313)
- (Lender note: At least one borrower must complete Homeownership Education) (Source: [[source-loandepot-conventional-guide-2026-03]], p.313)
- (Lender note: DU Approve/Eligible or LPA Accept/Eligible is required) (Source: [[source-loandepot-conventional-guide-2026-03]], p.313)

### FNMA-Specific Requirements

- (Lender note: Property must be 1-4 units) (Source: [[source-loandepot-conventional-guide-2026-03]], p.313)
- (Lender note: Reserves may be required as determined by DU) (Source: [[source-loandepot-conventional-guide-2026-03]], p.313)
- (Lender note: Nontraditional credit references may be required as determined by DU) (Source: [[source-loandepot-conventional-guide-2026-03]], p.313)

### FHLMC-Specific Requirements

- (Lender note: Property must be 1-unit only) (Source: [[source-loandepot-conventional-guide-2026-03]], p.313)
- (Lender note: Unless the last LPA feedback certificate indicates positive borrower cash flow was identified, nontraditional credit references are required) (Source: [[source-loandepot-conventional-guide-2026-03]], p.313)

## Ineligible Programs

(Lender note: The following programs are NOT eligible for no-credit-score loans:)
- HomeReady
- HomeStyle Renovation
- HomeStyle Energy
- Texas Homestead Refinance
- AccessOne
- HomeOne
- Home Possible

(Source: [[source-loandepot-conventional-guide-2026-03]], p.313)

## Interpretation Notes

⚠️ The nontraditional credit references mentioned here (when required by DU/LPA) are separate from voluntary nontraditional credit. See [[nontraditional-credit-history]] for documentation requirements when nontraditional credit references are needed.

⚠️ The 95% LTV cap is a loanDepot overlay. The underlying GSE guidelines may have different LTV limits — verify against FNMA Selling Guide or FHLMC Single-Family Guide before relying on this figure.
