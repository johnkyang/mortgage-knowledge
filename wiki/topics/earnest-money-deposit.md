## Summary

The deposit on the sales contract (earnest money) for the purchase of the security property is an acceptable source of funds for both down payment and closing costs. When used as part of the minimum borrower contribution, the source of the EMD must be verified. The EMD must not be double-counted (deducted from funds to close and simultaneously counted as an asset).

## Key Rules

### General

- Earnest money deposit is an eligible source of funds for down payment and closing costs. (Source: [[source-loandepot-conventional-guide-2026-03]])
- The EMD must **not** be counted twice — it may not be simultaneously deducted from funds to close and counted as an asset. (Source: [[source-loandepot-conventional-guide-2026-03]])

### FNMA

- When the deposit is part of the borrower's minimum contribution requirement, the source of funds must be verified.
- A Verification of Deposit (Fannie Mae Form 1006) must indicate the average balance for the past **two months** was large enough to support the deposit amount.
- Bank statements must evidence the two-month average balance was sufficient. If a canceled check is used, statements must cover through the date the check cleared.
- If funds cannot be confirmed as withdrawn from the borrower's account, additional verification is required showing the funds changed hands (from borrower to seller, realtor, escrow agent, or settlement attorney).
- Large earnest money deposits (or deposits exceeding customary local amounts) must be closely evaluated.
- Receipt must be verified by a copy of the borrower's canceled check **or** a written statement from the holder of the deposit. (Source: [[source-loandepot-conventional-guide-2026-03]])

### FHLMC

- For EMDs used to qualify the borrower, obtain evidence the EMD check cleared the borrower's account (e.g., canceled check, asset account statement, or written statement from EMD holder verifying receipt).
- When the EMD is needed to meet the **minimum contribution from personal funds**:
  - Verify the source is an eligible asset type and document per applicable requirements.
  - Provide account statements (Streamlined Accept or Standard requirements, as applicable) or a direct account verification covering through the date the EMD cleared. (Source: [[source-loandepot-conventional-guide-2026-03]])

## Exceptions & Nuances

- If the earnest money deposit comes from **gift funds**, the donor may provide the gift directly to a builder or real estate agent (not through the borrower's account). A signed gift letter plus evidence of fund transfer from the donor's financial institution is required. See [[gift-funds]].
