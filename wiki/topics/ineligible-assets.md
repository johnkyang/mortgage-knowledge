## Summary

All funds used to qualify the borrower (down payment, closing costs, reserves) must come from eligible sources. The following asset types are ineligible or restricted and may not be used unless specific conversion or exception conditions are met.

## Key Rules

### Ineligible Asset Types

| Asset Type | Ineligibility Details |
|-----------|----------------------|
| **Cash on Hand** | Ineligible except for HomeReady (FNMA) and Home Possible (FHLMC) programs per applicable guidelines. |
| **Cash-Out Refinance Proceeds** | Cash-out proceeds from the subject cash-out refinance, and any cash back on a "no cash-out" refinance, are ineligible for use as reserves. |
| **Credit Card Advances** | Credit card advances are ineligible except as described in [[credit-card-financing-reward-points]] (pre-closing application fees only, up to 2%/$1,500). |
| **Foreign Assets** | Funds held in foreign countries or foreign accounts are ineligible unless converted to U.S. dollars and deposited into an eligible asset account. FHLMC note: combined value of such converted assets must be at least 20% greater than the amount used for closing. |
| **Gifts of Equity and Personal Gifts** | Ineligible for investment property transactions. |
| **Gifts — Interested Party** | Personal gifts are ineligible when the donor is, or has any affiliation with, the builder, developer, real estate agent, or any other interested party to the transaction. |
| **Interested Party Contributions (IPCs)** | IPCs used for the borrower's down payment, reserves, or minimum borrower contribution requirements are ineligible. |
| **Margin Accounts** | Margin account funds are ineligible. Note: when determining the value of stocks and mutual funds, margin account values must be excluded. |
| **Retirement Accounts (restricted)** | Retirement accounts restricting the borrower's ability to make withdrawals or requiring severance from current employment are ineligible. |
| **Salary Advance** | Salary advance funds are ineligible. |
| **Loan Secured by Asset — from Interested Party** | Proceeds from a loan secured by the borrower's assets (other than real property) provided by an interested party to the real estate or mortgage transaction are ineligible. |
| **Stocks (restricted)** | Ineligible: stocks with limitations on accessibility, non-vested restricted stock units, non-vested stock options, stocks held in unlisted companies. |
| **Sweat Equity** | Sweat equity is an ineligible source of funds. |
| **UTMA/UGMA / Custodial Accounts** | Assets in a minor or custodial account (UTMA/UGMA) are ineligible unless the borrower is the beneficiary (minor who has reached adulthood) with unrestricted access. |
| **Unsecured Loans** | Signature loans and overdraft protection funds on checking accounts are ineligible. Exception: unsecured loans as part of an Employer Assistance Program or Freddie Mac Home Possible program may be acceptable if listed on the Affordable Housing Programs Directory. |

(Source: [[source-loandepot-conventional-guide-2026-03]])

### FHLMC — Additional Ineligible Sources for Second Homes

The following are ineligible for second home mortgages:
- Pooled funds
- Gift funds received as a wedding and/or graduation gift
- Gifts or grants from an agency
- Employer Assisted Homeownership (EAH) benefits

### FHLMC — Additional Ineligible Sources for Investment Properties

- Pooled funds

## Exceptions & Nuances

- If an otherwise ineligible asset is converted to U.S. dollars and deposited into an eligible account, the [[large-deposits]] section governs whether the source must be documented.
- HomeReady and Home Possible have expanded eligibility for certain restricted asset types (cash on hand, EAH benefits).
