_The loanDepot Conventional Guide does not contain a dedicated Foreign Income section. It references foreign income only as a tax return trigger (p. 145 FNMA: "uses foreign income to qualify"; p. 146 FHLMC: "employed income from a foreign source" and "non-employment/non-self-employment income from a foreign source"). Full foreign income guidelines must be sourced directly from the Fannie Mae Selling Guide (B3-3.1-09) or Freddie Mac Seller Guide (5305.2)._
